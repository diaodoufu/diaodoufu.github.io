# Doufu's first blog!

---

Over the past few days,I've learnt how to login [github](http://www.github.com/) and create my personal repository.But for a long time after that,I didn't start studying.However,I have never expected the optional course I chose,titled **"Open Source Software Development"** would require the use of *Github*.

---

第一篇blog就简单介绍并展示了*Markdown*的学习成果，后续内容也许会围绕：
- 个人小作品

- 学到的各种新知识
